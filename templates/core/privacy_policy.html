Data Protection & Security

PRIVACYPOLICY

Version 2.0 | Effective as of December 2025. Your data privacy is hardcoded into our system architecture.

01 INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect information that identifies you as a student, including your **Full Name**, **Mobile Number** (used as your primary account identifier), and **Email Address**. When you use our services, we also collect the document metadata necessary for the "PDF Analysis" engine to calculate your order total.

02 DOCUMENT RETENTION POLICY

Unlike traditional cloud storage, FastCopy operates on a **Non-Persistent Storage** model. Uploaded documents are encrypted during transit and stored in a temporary secure cache. All files are automatically wiped from our servers 24 hours after the order status is marked as "Delivered" or "Cancelled".

03 DATA ENCRYPTION

Your personal profile and order history are protected using **Industry-Standard Hashing (PBKDF2)**. We do not store plaintext passwords. Payment processing is handled by third-party encrypted gateways; we never see or store your credit card or UPI credentials.

Security First

Questions about your document safety? Reach out to our technical compliance team.
